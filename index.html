Appetizers

  • Caprese Salad

    Ripe tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, basil leaves, extra virgin olive oil, and balsamic glaze.

  • Burrata con Pomodorini

    Burrata cheese, cherry tomatoes, fresh basil, extra virgin olive oil.

  • Bruschetta al Pomodoro

    Grilled bread rubbed with garlic, tomatoes, basil, olive oil.

  • Antipasto Misto

    cured meats (salami, Prosciutto), cheeses (Parmigiano, Gorgonzola), marinated vegetables (olives, artichokes, roasted peppers), and bread

Main Courses

  • Osso Buco

    Veal shanks, white wine, broth, vegetables (carrots, celery, onions), and gremolata (parsley, lemon zest, garlic).

  • Saltimbocca

    Chicken cutlets, prosciutto, fresh sage, and butter.

  • Fritto Misto di Pesce

    shrimp, calamari, small fish

Desserts

  • Tiramisu

    Layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers and creamy mascarpone cheese, often dusted with cocoa powder.

  • Cannoli

    Crunchy pastry shells filled with a sweet and creamy ricotta cheese mixture, sometimes with chocolate chips or candied fruit.

  • Panna Cotta

    A simple, smooth, and creamy Italian dessert made from sweetened cream and gelatin, often served with a fruit coulis or caramel.

  • Affogato

    A scoop of vanilla gelato "drowned" with a hot, fresh shot of espresso, combining cold and hot elements for a bold flavor.

  • Semifreddo

    A semi-frozen dessert, similar to a mousse or parfait, often made with whipped cream and egg whites, offering a "half-cold" texture.

  • Biscotti

    Almond-flavored biscuits, typically hard and crunchy, perfect for dipping into coffee or Vin Santo.
